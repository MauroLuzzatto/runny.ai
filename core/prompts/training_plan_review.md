Review my training plan at a high level in the context of my race goal.

**Big picture assessment**:
- How many weeks until race day?
- What training phase should I be in right now? (base building, build, peak, taper)
- Am I on track for my goal based on current fitness (volume, paces, VO2max, race predictions)?

**Recent trajectory** (keep it brief):
- Is weekly volume trending in the right direction for this phase?
- Are key workouts (long runs, threshold, intervals) appearing at the right frequency?
- Any red flags? (stagnation, overtraining signs, missing workout types)

**Priorities for the coming 2-4 weeks**:
- What should I focus on most to stay on track?
- What can I afford to skip or reduce?
- Any phase transitions coming up (e.g. time to start sharpening, time to taper)?

Keep it high level — no individual workout details, just the strategic view. Think like a coach looking at the full training calendar, not a single session.
